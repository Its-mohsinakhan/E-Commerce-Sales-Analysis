Insight: ~20% customers contribute ~80% of revenue (Pareto effect) The Pareto Effect (also called the 80/20 Rule) states that: ~80% of outcomes come from ~20% of causes Its named after Vilfredo Pareto, an Italian economist. Pareto Effect in Data Analysis (Simple Meaning) In business datasets, it usually means: 80% of sales come from 20% of customers 80% of profit comes from 20% of products 80% of issues come from 20% of defects The exact numbers dont have to be exactly 80/20 its a pattern, not a rule.
